The sibling to the Spearmint Snail, this red and white sister creature follows much of the same behaviours.
The minor noticeable differences between the Peppermint Snail and the Spearmint Snail are the striking colour differences (red instead of green), and they do in fact smell slightly more like peppermint than spearmint.
This is one of the key reasons that historians believe that human candy was actually developed based off an accidental expedition to Candyland.
